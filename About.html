About us

 
 

As a company deeply rooted in both China and France, our journey began with a vision to revolutionize the smartphone industry by providing unparalleled access to cutting-edge technology at unbeatable prices. With offices strategically located in these two global hubs of innovation, we have been able to stay at the forefront of the ever-evolving mobile landscape, catering to customers worldwide with a diverse range of devices to suit every need and preference. However, as we look back on our journey, we find ourselves facing unexpected challenges that have led us to make the difficult decision to close our website. Despite our best efforts and unwavering dedication, external circumstances beyond our control have necessitated this action. Yet, in the face of adversity, we remain committed to our mission of delivering exceptional value to our customers. In light of our impending closure, we have made the bold decision to lower prices across our entire inventory and offer free shipping on all orders. This is our way of expressing gratitude to the countless loyal customers who have supported us over the years. We understand the importance of affordability and accessibility, especially during uncertain times, and we are determined to make our products more accessible than ever before. While the decision to close our website is undoubtedly bittersweet, we take solace in the knowledge that our legacy will live on through the countless individuals whose lives we have touched with our products and services.
